About FreeGive

FreeGive is a community-driven platform built on the simple idea that sharing should be effortless.

Our mission is to reduce waste and strengthen community bonds by providing a secure, reliable marketplace for neighbors to give and receive household items for free.

Our Vision

We envision a world where "disposable" is replaced by "sharable." By facilitating the transfer of items from those who no longer need them to those who do, we're building a more sustainable and connected society.

Safe Sharing

Every user on our platform is verified, and we encourage safe, public pickup locations. Your security and the integrity of our community are our top priorities.
